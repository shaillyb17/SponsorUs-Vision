Events

Noesis 6.0

NOESIS is the signature event of Vision. It was started in year 2011 and still continues to be the biggest technical event of the Institute after Technosearch, famously the Annual Technical Festival of the College. It is a 2-day mega technical event conducted in mid-september every year. In the past few years NOESIS received a great response. In year 2016, it was conducted on the 17th and the 18th September. Students from various colleges enthusiastically participated in great numbers. During NOESIS, usually branch specific technical workshops are organised so as to acquaint students with the recent advancements in their branches giving them an edge over with hands on projects.

Byte The Code

Byte the code is a workshop on python. In this workshop we aim to introduce students to the main structure and working of Python. This year it witnessed more than 400 participations. The highlight of the event was a project on web scrapping.

AutoCad

Were you ever good at drawing diagrams in the School? Ever thought of how the buildings or bridges are "Drawn"? Have a small glimpse of AutoCAD, which is a computer-aided drafting software program used to create blueprints for buildings, bridges, and computer chips, among other things.

Futurena

Have you ever wondered how a small digital watch on your wrist works?? Or ever pondered to enter in the world of technology and go beyond theory? So here comes Futurena, branch specific technical workshop organized in Noesis so as to acquaint students with the recent advancements in electronics circuits giving them an edge over with hands on projects in the workshop itself.

Aakriti

AAKRITI, a workshop organised to shape and mould the foundations of newly generated buds of CIVIL ENGINEERING. Its main focus was on creating an interactive session to enlighten Civil Engineering students about their branch and open up the new doors of excellence.

Asphentech

One of the workshops in Noesis, ASPEN-TECH is being organized every year. It's a chemical engineering software but anyone who is interested to learn, can attend the event. The event is totally student interactive.

Technosearch

Technosearch is the Annual Tech-Fest of NIT-BHOPAL which is held during the odd semester every year. The festival provides an opportunity to the many hundreds of aspiring young engineers to embrace success. Vision organizes various events on discrete fields be it electronics, electrical, coding, robotics, civil related events or even fun events. To name a few we had electrical and electronic based events like Circuitrun, Electrobuzz and Electrique; Civil based event, Cantilever; Coding events like Code Kriegers. We had a huge participation from colleges all over Bhopal.

Code Kriegers

In Technosearch, VISION brings to you another technical event Code Kriegers, an annual coding event organised every year. So, gear up for the next version of Code Kriegers. Brush up your CQ(Coding Quotient) to break down the clues, tackle a rapid-fire of snippets and then code your way to be called a 'Code Krieger'.

RoboFIFA

Love football and robot-making? Come to join us this Technosearch and enjoy Robo-FIFA which gives you an opportunity to do both at the same time! The Kriegers have to cripple the BOTMITES' system but they can't get in due to their defence. Take out your tools and build a BOT-Warrior to infiltrate the BOTMITES' and thrash their fusion chamber which is their defence's core.

Suspension Bridge

Another addition to the long list of technical events going to be held under Technosearch, The Last Stand conducted by Vision.Robots have made their place in every field out there. But you have to show that a machine is still a man's tool. Take your manual bots out on a walk. Wait! The road's not that easy mate. Get through the obstacles, clear the path and emerge as a 'Survivor'.

The Last Stand

Technosearch, NIT Bhopal presents another event by Vision, Suspension Bridge Model, a model making event.The Kriegers are on their way to plant the bug on the systems of BOTMITES but they have a problem. There's nothing that connects them to the BOTMITES' island. You need to work out your minds to design a suspension bridge model that can withstand the Kriegers on their BOT-warriors.
